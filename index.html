Hive
for iOS

Hive for iOS is the game of Hive that you love, optimized for your iPhone and iPad! Surround your opponent's queen bee, before they can surround yours.

Challenge your friends, or play against the Hive Mind AI. We support full offline or online play, using Sign in With Apple. Hive for iOS also supports two player games on a single device, so you can challenge your friends at home.

You can play with all 3 expansions: the lady bug, the mosquito, and the pill bug.
